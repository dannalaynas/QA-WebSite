Year Up

Year Up is a non-profit organization that helps close something called "The Opportunity and Divide". The Oportunity and Divide being the huge gap that separates talented individuals ages 18-25. They are given oportunities that aren't at their dispense. It is an intensive professional training program. Students are trained in a trainning room for 6 months learning everything they need to to be successful in the corperate enviorment. After 6 months they are sent off to an internship with one of Year Up's corperate partners for on hands learning.
